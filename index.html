Stop smoking for good!

Need a smoke? Stop. Breathe. Think.

Many people smoke when they have intense emotions. An emotional trigger reminds you how you felt when you used smoking to enhance a good mood or escape a bad one like stress or anxiety. Breathe allows you to learn how to cope with your feelings without leaning on cigarettes. Deep breathing will slow down your body, quiet your mind, and reduce cravings. So, let’s breathe!
